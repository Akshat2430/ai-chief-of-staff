> **What's wrong with this?**
> - Opens with "I hope this email finds you well" — a banned phrase
> - "Thank you for bringing this to our attention" — corporate filler
> - "Please don't hesitate to reach out" — another banned phrase
> - No concrete action, no timeline, no ownership
> - Didn't CC Marcus or Sarah (required for Hartwell)
> - Sounds like a support ticket auto-response, not a person
> - 140 words to say nothing specific
